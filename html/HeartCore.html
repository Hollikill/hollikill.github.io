HeartCore Datapack

Download Link (read installation instructions below)

HeartCore is a multiplayer-friendly semi-hardcore datapack designed to provide a fair experience between Hard and Hardcore difficulties. In HeartCore, each player has three lives, and lose 4 hearts with each life lost. However, by crafting an advanced golden apple referred to as a 'HeartCore', a player can regain a lost life. For recipes of each of the stages of a HeartCore, any player can use the /trigger info command in game. Players are set into spectator upon their third, and final, death. To use a HeartCore, simply drop it when you have lost a life. But there is another use for HeartCores! If you get a ghost to fly over a HeartCore on the ground, you can revive them from death.

Installation Instructions

For singleplayer, place a copy of HeartCore_datapack.zip into the datapacks folder in your world, and place a copy of HeartCore_textures.zip in your resource packs folder and enable it.

If you are using this on a server, make sure the resource-pack line in server.properties looks like this: resource-pack=https://hollikill.net/misc/HeartCore_textures.zip. Additionally, make sure that HeartCore_datapack.zip is in the datapacks folder and enabled enabled by going in game on your server and typing /datapack enable "HeartCore_datapack.zip"
