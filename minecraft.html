When you think of minecraft modding, the thing that comes to most people's head is the Forge modloader. While this is a quality launcher, and does it's job well, it simply cannot compare to the adaptability of the Fabric modloader. Fabric is a modloader designed to be leightweight and easy to update to new versions, created in 1.14. They update to new versions really fast. Like less than a week time even on the biggest stuff. It also has a lot more useful mods that work in the latest versions of minecraft.

If you want this amazing modloader, look no further than fabricmc.net
